THE MOMENTUM

90-DAY MARKETING & IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

From Code to Culture: A Comprehensive Launch Plan

EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

The launch of Castel-Wrld represents more than a technical upgrade—it's a cultural shift in how Malawians interact with their favorite beverage brands. This 90-day strategy combines digital virality with traditional grassroots engagement to ensure maximum adoption across all stakeholder groups: distributors, retailers, and consumers.

Our approach: Think Global, Act Local. We leverage social media's reach while maintaining the personal touch that defines Malawian business relationships.

THE 90-DAY ROADMAP

A phased approach that builds momentum from digital hype to physical presence, culminating in a market-wide celebration of Malawi's beverage industry evolution.

MONTH 1: DIGITAL HYPE

Social Media Skits & Viral Content

Week 1-2: Teaser Campaign

  • Launch mysterious countdown posts: "Something's brewing at Castel..."
  • Behind-the-scenes footage of "tech transformation"
  • Influencer partnerships with local comedians and content creators
  • Target: 50,000+ impressions across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok

Week 3-4: Educational Skits

  • Comedy sketches showing "old way vs. new way" of ordering
  • Animated explainer: "How Castel-Wrld Works in 60 Seconds"
  • Distributor testimonials (pre-launch beta users)
  • Interactive polls: "What feature excites you most?"
Key Deliverables: 12 video skits, 30 graphic posts, 3 influencer collaborations

MONTH 2: B2B ENGAGEMENT

Newspapers, Radio & Direct Outreach

Week 5-6: Traditional Media Blitz

  • Full-page newspaper ads in The Nation, Daily Times, Malawi News
  • Radio spots on Zodiak, Capital FM during peak commute hours
  • Press release: "Castel Malawi Leads Digital Transformation"
  • Trade magazine features targeting wholesale/retail sector

Week 7-8: Direct Distributor Onboarding

  • Personalized emails to all registered distributors
  • One-on-one video calls with top 20 partners
  • WhatsApp Business groups for regional support
  • Early adopter incentive: First 50 orders get 5% discount
Key Deliverables: 8 newspaper ads, 120 radio spots, 200+ distributor meetings

MONTH 3: THE LEGACY LAUNCH

Boots on the Ground & The Grand Bash

Week 9-10: Nationwide Roadshow

  • Northern Region: Mzuzu, Karonga - market visits, bar demos
  • Central Region: Lilongwe, Salima - distributor workshops
  • Southern Region: Blantyre, Zomba - retail activation
  • Branded vans with demo stations at major markets
  • Live order processing demonstrations

Week 11-12: The Grand Bash

  • Official launch event at Bingu International Convention Centre
  • Guest list: 200+ distributors, retailers, government officials, media
  • Keynote: "The Future of Beverage Distribution in Malawi"
  • Live platform demo with real-time order processing
  • Awards: "Top Digital Adopters" recognition
  • Entertainment: Local artists, DJ, catering by Castel brands
Key Deliverables: 15-city roadshow, 200-person launch event, national media coverage

RISK MITIGATION

  • Low Digital Literacy: In-person demos and video tutorials in Chichewa to ensure accessibility
  • Internet Connectivity: Platform optimized for 2G/3G speeds; offline capability for core features
  • Resistance to Change: Early adopter incentives and testimonials from trusted community leaders
  • Competition Response: First-mover advantage secured through aggressive 90-day push

CONCLUSION

This 90-day strategy transforms Castel-Wrld from a digital platform into a household name. By combining viral social media moments with the credibility of traditional media and the personal touch of on-ground activation, we ensure that every stakeholder—from the Blantyre wholesaler to the Mzuzu bar owner—understands, adopts, and advocates for the platform.

From code to culture. From launch to legacy.
