THE COMPACT

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Between: Development Team & Castel Malawi Ltd.

Effective Date: February 2026

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the terms, deliverables, timeline, and commercial commitments for the development and deployment of the Castel-Wrld Digital Ecosystem. This agreement ensures transparency, accountability, and mutual understanding between both parties.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The development team commits to delivering the following services:

  • Full-Stack Web Development: Complete design and development of a mobile-first, high-performance website with B2B ordering capabilities, asset tracking, and interactive mapping features.
  • Digital Brand Enhancement: Modernization of visual identity including logos, color schemes, and typography optimized for digital platforms.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Implementation of technical SEO best practices to ensure top search rankings for relevant keywords in the Malawian market.
  • System Integration: Secure connection of order forms to Castel's internal CRM or email systems with SSL encryption.
  • Training & Documentation: Comprehensive training for Marketing and I.T. teams, including written documentation for ongoing management.
  • Testing & Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing across multiple devices, browsers, and network conditions to ensure optimal performance.

3. PROJECT TIMELINE

The project will be completed in 4 weeks following this schedule:

Week 1: Content Alignment

  • Data migration and content porting
  • Pricing engine configuration
  • Geographic mapping setup
  • Milestone: Content structure approved

Week 2-3: System Integration

  • B2B order form development
  • Security implementation (SSL, authentication)
  • Mobile optimization and testing
  • Milestone: Functional prototype ready for review

Week 4: Launch & Training

  • Final testing and quality assurance
  • Live deployment to production domain
  • Staff training session
  • Milestone: Project completion and handover

4. INVESTMENT & PAYMENT TERMS

TOTAL INVESTMENT: MWK 1,800,000.00

PAYMENT PHASE AMOUNT PERCENTAGE TRIGGER
Commitment Fee MWK 900,000 50% Upon contract signing and project initiation
Completion Fee MWK 900,000 50% 30 days after live deployment and training completion

Payment Method: Bank transfer to designated business account (details to be provided upon signing).

5. DELIVERABLES INCLUDED

  • Website Platform: Fully functional, mobile-responsive website with all features outlined in the proposal
  • Source Code: Complete codebase transferred to Castel's repositories upon final payment
  • Design Assets: All logos, graphics, and brand materials in editable formats
  • Documentation: User manuals, technical documentation, and maintenance guides
  • Training: On-site or virtual training session (4 hours minimum)
  • Support Credits: 5 major updates or feature additions (valid for 3 years)
  • Consultancy: 12 months of strategic digital consultancy

6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Castel Malawi Ltd. agrees to provide:

  • Content & Data: All necessary text, images, pricing information, and distributor details within 3 business days of request
  • Timely Feedback: Review and approval of deliverables within 48 hours to maintain project timeline
  • Hosting Infrastructure: Domain registration and hosting services (recommendations will be provided)
  • Access Credentials: Necessary login credentials for CRM, email systems, and existing web infrastructure
  • Point of Contact: Designated project manager with decision-making authority

7. PERFORMANCE GUARANTEES

  • Page Load Speed: Average load time under 3 seconds on 3G networks
  • Mobile Compatibility: Fully functional on devices with Android 6.0+ and iOS 12+
  • Uptime: 99.5% uptime guarantee (subject to hosting provider SLA)
  • Security: SSL certificate implementation and secure data handling practices
  • Browser Support: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions)

8. POST-LAUNCH SUPPORT

Following the successful launch, the development team provides:

  • Bug Fixes: 30 days of complimentary bug fixes for issues arising from the initial build
  • Minor Updates: Unlimited content updates (text, images) for the first 60 days
  • Emergency Support: Priority response for critical issues (response within 4 hours)
  • Update Credits: 5 major feature updates or structural changes over 3 years
  • Annual Consultancy: Strategic advice on digital trends and optimization opportunities

9. CHANGE MANAGEMENT

Any changes to the agreed scope that require additional development time will be documented through a Change Request Form. Additional costs will be calculated at an hourly rate of MWK 25,000/hour and must be approved in writing before work commences.

10. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

  • Upon final payment, all custom code and design assets become the exclusive property of Castel Malawi Ltd.
  • Third-party libraries and frameworks remain under their respective open-source licenses
  • The development team retains the right to use the project in their portfolio (with client permission)

11. TERMINATION CLAUSE

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. In the event of termination:

  • All completed work up to the termination date will be delivered to the client
  • Payment for completed phases is non-refundable
  • Partial work in the current phase will be billed on a pro-rata basis
